Title: (Just the title of your app) 
---
Flatmates Bill

Description: (Describe what you want to create)
---
An app that gets as input the amount of a bill for a particular period
and the days that each of the flatmates stayed in the house for that period. Will 
return how much each flatmate has to pay. It also generates a PDF report stating the names of flatemates,
the period, and how much each of them had to pay.

Objects: (Use the nouns in your description to create objects)
---
Bill: 
	amount, period

Flatmate:
	days_in_house, name 
	pays()

PDFReport: 
	filename
	generate()
